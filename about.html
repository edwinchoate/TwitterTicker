About Us

TwitterTicker is an information visualization system developed by students for the Introduction to Information Visualization (CS 4460) course at Georgia Tech. For more information about the visualization itself, see the Help page.

We developed TwitterTicker because we were interested in learning more about the nature of the stock market and how good social media management can affect the financials of public corporations.
